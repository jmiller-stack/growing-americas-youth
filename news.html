Summer Program · 2025

Pumpkin Harvest 2025 — Summer Youth Program Recap

Twenty young people from across Baltimore City spent six weeks growing their own food, learning food safety, and building skills they'll carry for life. Each participant received their own hydroponic growing kit to take home — so the growing never stops. Older participants completed ServSafe Manager Certification training, earning a Maryland-recognized credential that opens doors to food service employment.

Produce grown included spinach, kale, butter crunch lettuce, basil, parsley, oregano, tomatoes, and thyme — all hydroponically, indoors, no soil required.
