What is Growing America's Youth?

If you can plant a seed,
you'll never be hungry.

We're a Baltimore-based nonprofit that teaches young people to grow food without soil — using hydroponics, the science of growing plants in water and nutrients. No farm required. No prior experience needed.

You'll walk away with hands-on skills, a ServSafe food safety certification, STEM knowledge, and real career experience. And yes — you get paid while you learn.

What you'll do

Our Programs

Real skills. Real certifications. Real outcomes. Here's what we offer:

🌿

Hydroponic Gardening

Learn to grow plants using water and nutrients — no soil needed. You'll plant seeds, manage grow systems, harvest produce, and understand the science behind it all. Every participant gets their own hydroponic growing kit to take home.

  • Hands-on
  • STEM
  • Take-home kit
📋

ServSafe Certification

Earn your ServSafe Manager Certification — a nationally recognized food safety credential required by restaurants, grocery stores, and food service employers across Maryland. You need a 75% or higher to pass. We'll prepare you to nail it.

  • Ages 18+
  • Job-ready
  • Maryland recognized
🔬

STEM Skills

Hydroponics is science. You'll work with pH levels, nutrient solutions, LED grow lights, and plant biology. Our curriculum connects what you're growing to math, chemistry, and environmental science in a way that actually makes sense.

  • Science
  • Math
  • Technology
🍳

Food Safety & Nutrition

Learn what's in your food, how to handle it safely, and how to cook with what you grow. We believe knowing how to feed yourself is one of the most powerful skills you can have.

  • Cooking
  • Nutrition
  • Food safety
💼

Life Skills & Career Development

Resume writing. Interview skills. Professional communication. Financial basics. We help you build the foundation for whatever comes next — college, a job, or starting your own thing.

  • Career ready
  • Resume
  • Interview prep
☀️

Summer Youth Program

A 6-week summer cohort for Baltimore youth. You'll plant, grow, harvest, and cook every week. Older participants get full ServSafe training and certification. Everyone gets a $25/week stipend through the Summer Youth Employment Program and a hydroponic grow kit to keep at the end.

  • 6 weeks
  • $25/week stipend
  • Free kit included

Why it matters

Food is power.
We're putting it in your hands.

🏙️ Baltimore's food deserts are real.

Many neighborhoods in Baltimore City have limited access to fresh, affordable produce. We're not waiting for the system to fix it — we're teaching young people to grow their own, right here, indoors, year-round.

💧 Hydroponics uses 90% less water.

Hydroponic farming is one of the most sustainable ways to grow food on the planet. You're not just learning a skill — you're learning how to feed communities in a way that doesn't destroy the planet in the process.

🎓 Certifications open doors.

A ServSafe certification isn't a piece of paper — it's a key. Restaurants, cafeterias, grocery chains, and food businesses in Maryland require it. We help you earn it for free.

🌱 From seed to career.

The skills you learn here — growing, food safety, STEM, professional development — connect to real jobs in urban farming, food service, environmental science, and entrepreneurship.

Who we are

Built for Baltimore.
By people who believe in Baltimore.

Growing America's Youth was founded in 2018 with one belief: if you can plant a seed, you'll never be hungry. We started in Baltimore City because that's where the need is — and that's where the people are.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Our programs are free. Our staff is invested in you. And everything we do is designed to give Baltimore youth more options, more skills, and more power over their own futures.

We serve youth and families across Baltimore City and surrounding Maryland counties — including families, schools, community organizations, and young people who just need a place to start.

Questions

Real talk

Is this free?

Yes. 100% free for all participants. No cost, no catch.

Do I really get paid?

Summer cohort participants receive a $25/week stipend through the Summer Youth Employment Program.

What do I need to bring?

Yourself and a willingness to learn. We provide all materials, equipment, and supplies.

What age do I need to be?

Programs are designed for youth ages 13–24. ServSafe certification is open to participants 18 and older.

Where are you located?

We serve Baltimore City and surrounding Maryland counties. Contact us for current program locations.

Do I need experience?

No experience with growing, farming, or science required. We teach you everything from scratch.

What will I walk away with?

Hands-on hydroponic skills, your own grow kit, potential ServSafe certification, career development skills, and a network of people who have your back.

How do I sign up?

Email us at mjosey@growingamericasyouth.org or hit "Apply Now" above. We'll get back to you within 48 hours.
